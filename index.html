The physics

Why light bends

Every refraction follows the same three-step story. Change the medium or angle in the simulator below and watch it play out in real time.

01 — CONTACT
Light meets a boundary
A ray travelling through one medium arrives at the surface of another — air into water, air into glass, glass into diamond.
02 — SPEED CHANGE
Its speed changes
Denser media slow light down. The ratio of the two speeds is exactly the ratio of refractive indices, n₂/n₁.
03 — BENDING
The path bends
To conserve the wavefront, the ray changes direction — toward the normal entering a denser medium, away from it entering a lighter one.
